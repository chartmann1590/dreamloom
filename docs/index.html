What it does

Not a chatbot. A private instrument.

The app is intentionally narrow: dream capture, interpretation, reflection, search, and weekly patterns. Nothing social. Nothing performative.

01

02

03

Privacy promise

Your dreams are not content.

They are not prompts for a cloud service, not training material, not a social feed, and not data to sell. Dreamloom treats the journal as something intimate by default.

First launch note

The local AI model is downloaded once during setup and needs several gigabytes of free storage. After that, the dream journal can run offline.

Questions

Plain answers.

Is this therapy?

No. Dreamloom is a journal and reflection tool, not medical, mental health, or therapeutic advice.

Does it need the internet?

Setup needs network access for the model download. Core journaling and interpretation run locally afterward.

Is it actually free?

Yes. Dreamloom has no subscription, premium tier, free trial, or in-app purchases.
